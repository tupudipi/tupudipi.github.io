Travel to .... Tuscany!

If you didn't want to, you sure will after seeing this page!

Reasons you should:

  • good wine
  • great views
  • italian food

Things you should do when you get there:

  1. Go to a restaurant and order all the italian dishes starting with the best lasagna you will ever have.
  2. Drink a ton of wine.
  3. Drive around at sunset.

I'd suggest waiting for the global pandemic to calm down, though. The vineyards aren't going anywhere.
